Magnetics

MPS Industries, Inc. is a leading manufacturer of electronic components, specializing in custom transformers, inductors, common mode chokes, current sensors and power supplies. We offer our clients a broad product line, custom design capabilities, quality products and services, local U.S. based design and manufacturing support, and overseas production and shipping.

Offering a broad product line and custom design, MPS Industries, Inc. covers a wide array of industry applications, including automotive, industrial, medical, military and aerospace, telecom, and consumer electronics. Our line of magnetic components includes custom transformers, inductors, current sensors, common mode chokes, and filters. Our magnetic component offerings range in size from large EI lamination or toroidal transformers to tiny surface mount inductors. We also distribute wall mount, open frame, desktop, and industrial power supplies, including medical grade versions.
