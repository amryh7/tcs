Alpha Circuit Corporation (ACC) was formed by Bhagvan K Vaghani in 1981 at the onset of the printed circuit board (PCB) fabrication boom and continued under his ownership through 2012.  At the beginning of 2013, ACC management responsibility was handed over to Yash Sutariya (formerly of Saturn Electronics) to position the company for a next generation.

After more than 30 years of service, Alpha Circuit Corporation and Saturn Flex Systems combined to form a new generation. In June 2013, Yash merged his Saturn Flex Systems, Inc. operation into ACC, making ACC a full service provider of PCBs ranging from standard technology rigid, through rigid-flex, HDI, and exotic builds.

The goal at Saturn Flex is to be the easiest PCB supplier to work with.  Part of achieving this goal is through obtaining relevant industry certifications. These include TS16949 (Automotive - completed July 2013), AS9100 (Aerospace - est. Feb. 2014), and MIL-Prf-31032 (Military - est. Dec. 2014).

Currently, Saturn Flex is the 1st Rigid-Flex, 2nd Flex, and 4th Rigid PCB manufacturer in North America to hold TS16949.  They are the only PCB fabricator to hold this certification and offer a complete range of technology solutions.
