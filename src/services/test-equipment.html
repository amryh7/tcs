test equipment

Established in 2008, TestWorld Inc. grew through the global recession by providing good old fashioned customer service. It’s easy to find a deal on the internet these days, it is hard to find a reliable company that will support it’s customers after an order. TestWorld customer service representatives work closely with it’s customer base and does what is needed to get the right solution to a customer in a time effective manner.

Immediately following incorporation, TestWorld established a division providing used and refurbished networking and IT equipment, known as TechWorld Networks. In 2012, TestWorld relocated to it’s current facility in Rocklin, CA with 50,000 Square Feet for warehouse and stocking space. Tentative plans include expanding repair and calibration capabilities and on-site services. Additional divisions planned to open include RF, EMC and electrical testing.

Establishing an account with Net Terms is a great way to get started. TestWorld representatives will respect your time if you respect theirs. Priority is given to established customers and potential customers will be given utmost attention (you’ll be needed as we grow).
