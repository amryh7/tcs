welcome to ‘the component store’ (tcs)

‘the component store’ (TCS) is one of the leading technology sales groups in North America. We specialize in the procurement of quality printed circuits for rigid, flex & rigid-flex boards. Whether it’s for quick turns, small volumes & large production runs, we can accommodate your PCB requirements. As an added value, we manufacture polymer pcb stencils for our customer’s prototyping needs.

the component store’ also offers a magnetics line. We manufacture custom transformers & inductors for many companies…..hence our motto, “Simply Resourceful”! We are engaged with customers on a daily basis that have a need for a reliable vendor who can fulfill their hardware needs. In this industry of electronics manufacturing, engineers are heavily pressed for time sensitive projects. From concept-to-market, they have deadlines to meet with no room for error. Their success is attributed with the support of a solid supplier base. We look forward to receiving your next quote request for PCBs.
