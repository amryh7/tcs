welcome to ‘the component store’ (tcs)

‘the component store’ is a highly recognized leader in the electronics manufacturing industry serving the needs of engineering professionals throughout North America. We specialize in supplying test, measurement & telecom equipment to various markets. Whether you’re seeking new or refurbished equipment, equipment leases or rentals, or you have equipment sell or trade-in, we can fulfill your hardware needs. We also offer certified calibration services to the automotive, medical and aerospace/defense industry.

tcs prides itself on being 'Simply Resourceful' for each of its valued clients. Striving for excellence in customer service is our top priority, and is critical to our future success. At tcs, we endeavour to make ourselves simply resourceful" to each of our valued clients. Get in touch with us for a "Quick Quote" on just about anything for your test & measurement needs.
